# **Journalit is out of beta (1.6.5)**

Thank you to everyone who has helped test Journalit over the past few months. Your bug reports, feedback, and feature ideas have made a real difference.

Journalit is now out of beta, and paid plans are now available.

## **Freemium model**
At launch, the following are Pro features:
- **CSV import**
- **Trade Sync** (MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5)

Everything else remains free, including manual trade entry, dashboards, review notes and templates, account management, and the trade log.

## **Pricing (standard)**
- **$18/month**
- **$180/year**

## **Installation and updates**
Journalit will continue to be installed and updated via **BRAT** for now.

---

# **What’s new:**
New import options, smoother first-run experience, and more accurate stats across the dashboard and home widgets.

## **XLSX / XLS Imports**
Import broker exports without converting to CSV first.
- **Excel support** - Upload `.xlsx` / `.xls` files directly in the trade importer

## **Onboarding Revamp + Preview-First CSV Import**
Redesigned the onboarding. `Ctrl + P` and search "Replay Onboarding" to view it

## **Better Error UX + Support Reports**
If you have any import or sync errors, you can now easily copy a report and send it to #bugs

## **Breakeven Win Rate + Home Widgets Accuracy**
Breakeven trades are now handled consistently across the app.
- **Win rate fix** - Breakevens no longer count in the win-rate denominator
- **Streak fix** - Current streaks no longer break on breakeven trades
- **Weekly summary visuals** - Breakeven days render as neutral (not “green days”)

## **Support for themes**
Journalit now adapts to your themes styling

+ quite a bit of cleanup and fixes
