# **[1.8.0] - 2026/07/13**

New Setups analytics, Session Mode, Gallery, and trade workflow customization.

### **Setups + Playbooks**

![Setups](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/afada6bd-1a26-4133-a7af-24607069699a)
Build structured setups and understand your edge.

- Create setups with rules, linked playbook notes, status, and custom colors
- Review P&L, R-multiple, drawdown, execution gaps, setup pairs, and performance breakdowns
- Compare setups across instruments, weekdays, directions, and more

### **Session Mode**

![Workflow](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/99baa272-fb18-46a9-8930-4a4e574b1699)
A focused workspace for every stage of your session.

- Preparation, Waiting, Break, Live, and Ended views
- **Session Log** — Capture analysis, trades, psychology, market notes, and lessons
- **Trade Gate** — Build decision checks for trade execution
- Revisit session activity and lessons inside reviews

### **Gallery + Media Support**

![Galery](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8efc70d6-d0aa-4cb0-9f13-497dc8957ca4)
Review media from trades and journal reviews in one place.
_Access via Trade Log → Gallery_

- Filter and annotate media across your journal
- View images, GIFs, videos, Excalidraw files, and YouTube links
- Use fullscreen video controls or right-click an image to copy it
